ABOUT US

Welcome To Hotel J ESVI In The Andheri Suburb of Mumbai!

One of the best budget luxury hotels in Mumbai, Hotel J ESVI offers fully Air Conditioned rooms, en-suite bathrooms, 24/7 Reception, free-wifi, breakfast, mini bar and a heavenly bed.

The spacious hotel rooms have options for single & double occupancy and are equipped with best in class modern amenities like LED TV with choice of cable channels, mini bar and free Wi-Fi.

Our commuter friendly location on S.V. Road near the railway station and Bus Station ensures that our guests are able to access every corner of Mumbai using public transportation.

We serve hot breakfast with tea & coffee and choice of eggs. There is a McDonald’s restaurant on the Ground Floor of our Hotel. Dominos and Subway are walking distance away and there are several famous Indian restaurants like Delhi Darbar, Café Arfa, Delhi Zaika, Persia Darbar within a 10 minute walking distance from our hotel. Our guests frequently order food delivery to the hotel and we are happy to serve it with our plates and cutlery.

We take pride the hospitality of our team that strives everyday to make our guests feel comfortable & cared for.

Why Choose Us

Perfect for Exhibitions

Our hotel is top-rated among hotels near Bombay Exhibition Centre NESCO, Goregaon. Even during peak traffic hours, guests can save time by taking a 5-minute train ride to Goregaon Station or using the new Mrinal Tai Gore flyover which brings you right to NESCO. Because we are near a public transportation hub (train, bus & highway) guests can access any corner of Mumbai using public transportation.

Ideal for Film & T.V. Production Crew

We are just 2.5 km close to the main hub of Mumbai’s Media & Entertainment Industry in Veera Desai, Andheri West with companies as Yashraj Studios, Balaji Telefilms, Dharma Productions, & Reliance Entertainment.

Comfortable for Medical Tourism

The hotel is a preferred hotel for medical tourism and is the perfect choice for families who are looking for hotels near Kokilaben Hospital (4.2 km) & Nanavati Hospital (4.6 km). We have wheelchair and IV stands in-house for those recovering from surgeries and our staff has been trained to look after them.

Safe for Women & International Tourists

A Security Guard and 24*7 Camera Surveillance At Public Areas/floor with two-week backup. In-room Telephone assistance is available. Room to be allocated near the elevator/emergency exit (subject to availability). There is a Knock and Drop Service for food and beverages. Here we assure you of a smooth check-in process. A Well-lit outskirts area and main road access outside the hotel. In all rooms, we have double locks and a Door viewer.
