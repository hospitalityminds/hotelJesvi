ABOUT US

Welcome To Hotel J ESVI In The Andheri Suburb of Mumbai!

Hotel J ESVI is one of the best budget luxury hotels in Andheri, Mumbai. Hotel J ESVI is located on S.V. Road, near Jogeshwari Railway station (140 m). The closest railway station is Jogeshwari Railway station (140 m) and the Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport is 8.5 km or 18 minutes by cab. This 3-star budget hotel in Jogeshwari offers 30 spacious rooms. Hotel J ESVI offers fully Air Conditioned rooms, en-suite bathrooms, 24/7 Reception, free wifi, breakfast, minibar, and a heavenly bed which is perfect for corporate and family stays. The spacious hotel rooms have options for single & double occupancy and are equipped with best-in-class modern amenities like LED TV with a choice of cable channels, mini bar, and free Wi-Fi.

Our commuter-friendly location on S.V. Road near the Jogeshwari railway station and Jogeshwari Bus Station ensures that our guests can access every corner of Mumbai using public transportation. We are located in the heart of Mumbai Bombay Exhibition Centre is just 3kms away.We serve hot breakfast with tea & coffee and a choice of eggs. There is a McDonald’s restaurant on the Ground Floor of our Hotel.
Dominos and Subway are walking distance away and there are several famous Indian restaurants like Delhi Darbar, Café Arfa, Delhi Zaika, Persia Darbar within a 10-minute walking distance from our Hotel J ESVI. Our guests frequently order food delivery to the hotel and we are happy to serve it with our plates and cutlery. We take pride in the hospitality of our team that strives every day to make our guests feel comfortable & cared for.

Why Choose Us

Perfect for Exhibitions

Hotel J ESVI is top-rated among hotels near Bombay Exhibition Centre NESCO, Goregaon. Even during peak traffic hours, guests can save time by taking a 5-minute train ride to Goregaon Station or using the new Mrinal Tai Gore flyover which brings you right to NESCO. Because we are near a public transportation hub (train, bus & highway) guests can access any corner of Mumbai using public transportation.

Ideal for Film & T.V. Production Crew

Hotel J ESVI is just 2.5 km from the main hub of Mumbai’s Media & Entertainment Industry in Veera Desai, Andheri West with companies as Yashraj Studios, Balaji Telefilms, Dharma Productions, & Reliance Entertainment. Our hotel is very close to Lokhandwala Complex & Juhu where most of the films & T.V. Actors live and work.

Comfortable for Medical Tourism

Hotel J ESVI is a preferred hotel for medical tourism and is the perfect choice for families who are looking for hotels in Jogeshwari near Kokilaben Hospital (4.2 km) & Nanavati Hospital (4.6 km). We have a wheelchair and IV stands in-house for those recovering from surgeries and our staff has been trained to look after them.

Safe for Women & International Tourists

We have a 24/7 front desk, security guards & camera surveillance throughout the property with a two-week video backup. Our property is fire compliant with smoke & fire alarms and a comprehensive fire-fighting system that is audited every six months. We assure you of a smooth check-in process. Rooms can be allocated near the elevator/emergency exit on request and subject to availability. In all rooms, we have double locks and a door viewer. You may request a knock and drop service for food and beverages. The hotel compound is well lit and the main S.V. road is right outside the hotel.
