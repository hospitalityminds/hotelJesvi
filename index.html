ABOUT US

WELCOME TO J ESVI Hotel In Jogeswari

One of the best hotels in Jogeshwari West, Hotel J ESVI is a complete budget-friendly family Hotel in Jogeshwari, for both Business and Leisure Travellers situated in the heart of Mumbai City. This hotel is a budget-oriented hotel in Jogeshwariwest with 37 spacious rooms.

Hotel J ESVI, one of the best hotel in Mumbai offers fully Air Conditioned rooms and is well equipped with best in class modern amenities like LED TV, free Wi-Fi and much more . Our hotel is also well equipped with CCTV surveillance and smoke Fire alarms for safety & Security of our valuable guest.
