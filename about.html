Where We Are Located

Hotel J ESVI is located on S.V. Road, near Jogeshwari Railway station (140 m).The closest railway station is Jogeshwari Railway station (140 m) and the ChhatrapatiShivaji International Airport is 8.5 km or 18 minutes by cab.

Our Rooms and Amenities

Hotel J ESVI provides modern designed, multi-amenity rooms, with wooden furnishings and cushions. All rooms are well-equipped with amenities like spotless linens, LCD TVs, wardrobe, mineral water bottles, chair and table, and attached bathroom with 24-hour running hot and cold water. The hotel provides facilities such as parking, housekeeping, 24×7 security, 100% power backup, and free Wi-Fi access.

Where to Eat

Guests can enjoy complimentary Indo-Continental breakfast at the hotel or order sumptuous meals to their rooms via room service.Foodies can find plenty of restaurants and cafes nearby such as Hotel Bostan, Delhi Zaika, Persia Darbar,Farmik, Oza Sweets, and Farsan.
