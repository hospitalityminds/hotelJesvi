About Us

WELCOME TO J ESVI

One of the best budget luxury hotels in Mumbai, Hotel J ESVI offers fully Air Conditioned rooms, en- suite bathrooms, 24/7 Reception, free-wifi, breakfast, mini bar and a heavenly bed.

The 31 spacious hotel rooms have options for single & double occupancy and are equipped with best in class modern amenities like LED TV with choice of cable channels, mini bar and free Wi-Fi.

Why Choose Us

Our hotel is top-rated among hotels near Bombay Exhibition Centre NESCO, Goregaon and is also reasonably close to the new JIO Convention Center.

We are just 2.5 kms close to the main hub of Mumbai’s Media & Entertainment Industry in Veera Desai, Andheri West with companies as Yashraj Studios, Balaji Telefilms, Dharma Productions, Reliance Entertainment, ITOP Productions.

Bringing happiness and prosperity to the hotel and our guests. Designed for your comfort, we are the perfect solution for anyone searching for a cost-effective luxury & Corporate Service hotel in Mumbai near Andheri suburb.

Hotel J ESVI is very safe for independent women travellers and is equipped with 24/7 CCTV surveillance, smoke & fire alarms and a comprehensive fire-fighting system that is audited every six months.
Because of our value pricing we have become very popular with international tourists who are on a travel budget and need to stay at a hotel that’s safe, comfortable and pocket friendly.
